About Us

MANARA is a network of services with a mission to create sustainable value that stimulates local economic and social

development in Algeria.

We rely on entrepreneurial approach and engage with multiple stakeholders in the projects we undertake.

SERVICES

THE GATE

THE GATE is a service offering that supports foreign companies establish presence in Algeria.
Our role is to empower clients to make insightful decisions prior to committing any significant investment. Proceeded by jointly aligning strategy, action plan, required resources, and relevant KPIs. Our objective is to support you in this process and ensure execution excellence.

THE FACTORY

THE FACTORY, a MANARA venture specializing in launching spinoff startups and managing projects that offer innovative solutions to problems deemed critical by society and private sector.

THE UPLIFT

THE UPLIFT is a rolling program for startups, whether it is for idea validation at pre-seed level or scaling the business for robust growth. The one common denominator in both cases is MANARA presence as a valued equity partner. Through the program we provide our enrolled startups with office space, learning opportunities, structured working methodology and framework, and access to a broad network of experts and investors.
